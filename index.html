🚀 SHARE WITH YOUR TEAM — Deploy to GitHub Pages (free) ▼ show

No installs needed — just a free GitHub account. Everyone on your team gets a shareable URL.

Step 1 — Go to github.com and sign in (or create a free account).
Step 2 — Click the + icon (top right) → New repository.
Name it anything, e.g. my-tasks. Set it to Public. Click Create repository.
Step 3 — On the new repo page, click uploading an existing file.
Drag and drop task-manager.html into the box.
⚠️ Rename it to index.html before uploading (GitHub Pages looks for this name).
Click Commit changes.
Step 4 — Go to your repo's Settings tab → Pages (left sidebar).
Under Branch, select main and click Save.
Done! ✓ — After ~30 seconds your app is live at:
https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/my-tasks/
Share that URL with your team — no installs, no logins, works in any browser.
💡 Each person connects their own Jira & Microsoft accounts — credentials are stored in their own browser only, never shared or visible to others.
